Conservation of Tribal Paintings

PRESERVING HERITAGE, EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES

We are committed to fostering sustainable livelihood opportunities for underprivileged communities while preserving indigenous art forms. Our initiative focuses on safeguarding the vibrant traditional wall art of the Santhal tribal community, ensuring its cultural legacy endures. By supporting and promoting this artistic heritage, we empower Santhal youth with economic opportunities, blending tradition with sustainability for a brighter future.
